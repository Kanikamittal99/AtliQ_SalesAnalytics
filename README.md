## üíº Business Metrics Used:
1. **Net Sales**: Total revenue generated by the company.
2. **COGS (Cost of Goods Sold):** Direct costs associated with producing or purchasing goods sold. For Example: Manufacturing Overhead, freight costs, etc.
3. **Gross Margin:** Difference between net sales and COGS. Indicates how efficiently a company is producing goods and services, as well as its ability to cover operating expenses and generate profits.
4. **Gross Margin%:** Gross Margin expressed as a percentage of net sales. Indicates how efficiently a company is producing goods and services, as well as its ability to cover operating expenses and generate profits.
